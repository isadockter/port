UI/UX & HCC

EcoU

EcoU is designed to address the critical issue of recycling accessibility and education. We simply recycling by providing city regulations, comphrensive recycling information, and a map feature showcasing nearby recycling centers and bins.

Along with an innovative Scan feature that identifies unknown items and offered detailed information. Throughout the design process, we conducted extensive user testing with methods such as Card Sort and Wizard of Oz tests. We ultimately decided to add a rewards that incentivises the user by earning points for recycling engagement.

Figma/Miro/DataScraper

Online Shopping

StyleBytes is reshaping the online shopping experience by offering a personalized and inclusive platform. Focused on addressing the overwhelming nature of online shopping, we created a random outfit generator to help users discover their unique style.

The design process involved iterative development, going from Low to Medium to High fidelity prototypes based on feedback during user tests.

By allowing users to filter clothes by style and size, we aim to provide a stress-free and enjoyable online shopping experience, tailored to each individual users' preferences.
