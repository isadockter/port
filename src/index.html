Web Dev/Design

Healthy Bytes

In my ongoing senior capstone project, HealthyBytes, I contribute to the development of a responsive & user friendly web app aimed to help college students make healthier choices within budget constraints and busy schedules.

During the 1st half of this project, we conducted various research methods to fully understand our target audience. Despite being a work in progress, our goal is to create a valuable resource for students seeking healthier lifestyles.

HTML/CSS, Bootstrap, JS - PHP, MySQL

Outdoor Adventures

Outdoor Adventures is a trip management application that enables users to seamlessly add, edit, and delete trips, as well as manage members. We started out project with a CSV-based database, but later transitioned to using SQL and structured tables to better suit our requirements.

Along with the functionality, I included robust error validation on all inputs to ensure database security. I gained a solid foundation of web development with this project, especially with routing and working with frameworks.

HTML/CSS, Bootstrap - Python, Flask, MySQL

UI/UX & HCC

EcoU

EcoU is designed to address the critical issue of recycling accessibility and education. We simply recycling by providing city regulations, comphrensive recycling information, and a map feature showcasing nearby recycling centers and bins.

Along with an innovative Scan feature that identifies unknown items and offered detailed information. Throughout the design process, we conducted extensive user testing with methods such as Card Sort and Wizard of Oz tests. We ultimately decided to add a rewards that incentivises the user by earning points for recycling engagement.

Figma, Miro Board, DataScraper

Online Shopping

StyleBytes is reshaping the online shopping experience by offering a personalized and inclusive platform. Focused on addressing the overwhelming nature of online shopping, we created a random outfit generator to help users discover their unique style.

The design process involved iterative development, going from Low to Medium to High fidelity prototypes based on feedback during user tests.

By allowing users to filter clothes by style and size, we aim to provide a stress-free and enjoyable online shopping experience, tailored to each individual users' preferences.

Figma, Miro Board
